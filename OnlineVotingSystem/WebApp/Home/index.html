General Rules

  • Voters must undergo verification by the administrator before they can cast their vote.
  • The administrator is responsible for adding candidate details provided by the candidates for the election.
  • Each voter is allowed to cast only one vote in any given election.
  • Voters have access to basic information about the candidates and their agendas.
  • Neither candidates, voters, nor the administrator can ascertain which voter has voted for a specific candidate.
  • The results of the election are only accessible to voters after the election has concluded.
  • In the event of a tie between candidates, a runoff election may be held to determine the winner.
